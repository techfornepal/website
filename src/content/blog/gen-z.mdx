September 8 and 9 of 2025 have arguably been the most significant days in the history of Nepal since
the overthrow of the monarchy in 2006; however, this one has a different air about it. Nepali youth
were in the center stage of the protests against the government for these 2 days, but this has
radicalized the whole country like never before, especially after more than 20 students, some of
whom were still teenagers, were killed. No more political parties, no more the same 84 year-olds
running the country. Just new faces, new ideas, new hope.

> Revolution starts with the youth.
